Yumi ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Yumi Discord bot ("Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you interact with Yumi.
1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

    User Data: Basic identifiers such as Discord username, ID, and messages you send to Yumi.
    Feedback: Reactions and feedback data, such as thumbs up or thumbs down on Yumi's responses.

We do not intentionally collect personal information beyond what is necessary for the functionality of Yumi.
2. How We Use Your Information

    To provide responses and improve the user experience of Yumi.
    To store feedback for future improvements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to ensure proper functionality and security of Yumi.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
5. Your Choices

You can stop Yumi from collecting further information by ceasing interactions with the bot or by leaving the server Yumi is operating in.
